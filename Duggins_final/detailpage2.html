Accessories

What are PC Accessories?

Gaming accessories can be anything from mouses to microphones. If you spend enough money they can make your experience so much better, but it's a dpuble edged sword. If you don't spend enough money you can end up with crappy headphones that release painful static into your ears.

The Importance of Accessories

Gaming accessories are one of the most important aspects of gaming. Having awesome RGB and all the bells and whistles can make your pc look good but I would suggest investing in a better keyboard and mouse. Having a crappy keyboard and mouse can stop you from clutching up and make you look like a loser.
